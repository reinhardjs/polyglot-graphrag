# BUG-204: Checkout cascade timeout during peak traffic

- Reported by: bob
- Component: checkout service
- Severity: high
- Status: fixed (see PR-482)

## Summary

During Black Friday peak, the checkout service timed out because the payments
call was made synchronously inside the order write path. A slow PostgreSQL
replica caused a cascade: orders piled up, the connection pool exhausted, and
checkout failed for ~12% of users for 8 minutes.

## Root cause

The checkout service called the payments service inline (no timeout budget),
and the payments service downstream called PostgreSQL with a 30s default
statement timeout. Under load the replica lagged, statements queued, and the
pool saturated.

## Impact

- 12% checkout failure rate for 8 minutes.
- bob (on-call) paged; carol (db lead) killed long-running statements.
