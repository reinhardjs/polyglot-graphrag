# PR-482: Make payments call async with a bounded timeout budget

- Author: dave
- Fixes: BUG-204
- Reviewer: carol
- Status: merged

## What changed

- The checkout service now enqueues the payment request to a queue instead of
  calling the payments service synchronously.
- Added a 2s timeout budget on the payments call; on timeout the order is
  marked `pending_payment` and reconciled later by the billing service.
- Switched the payments service PostgreSQL connection to use PgBouncer and a
  5s statement timeout.

## Result

Eliminates the connection-pool cascade that caused BUG-204. Checkout failure
rate returned to baseline (<0.1%) in staging under 3x peak load.

## Linked

- Relates to ADR-021 (PostgreSQL primary store).
- Validated by the checkout runbook load test.
